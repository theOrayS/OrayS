## Full stable706 sweep caveat

A full RV stable sweep was executed after updating the list to 706:

```bash
OSCOMP_TEST_GROUPS=ltp LTP_CASES=stable LTP_CASE_TIMEOUT_SECS=45 timeout 80m ./run-eval.sh rv
python3 scripts/ltp_summary.py target/ltp-1000-milestone-04-stable706/rv-stable706-final-gate-20260602T205711+0800.log \
  > target/ltp-1000-milestone-04-stable706/rv-stable706-final-gate-20260602T205711+0800.log.summary.txt
```

Parser result: `PASS LTP CASE: 1393`, `FAIL LTP CASE: 19`, internal `{'TCONF': 4, 'TBROK': 4, 'TFAIL': 36}`, timeout `2`, ENOSYS `0`, panic/trap `0`.

This full RV sweep is **not** used as promotion evidence. Several failures are legacy/long-sequence blockers outside the new50 proof boundary; a targeted repair then made `clock_gettime04` and `copy_file_range03` clean in `rv-stable706-clock-copy-finalfix-20260602T221137+0800.log`, but `mmap10`, `mmap-corruption01`, and `test_ioctl` remain disclosed full-stable blockers. Because the RV full stable sweep was not clean, a full LA stable sweep was not used for this milestone. Future milestones must fix or isolate these blockers before making any final stable1000/full-suite claim.
