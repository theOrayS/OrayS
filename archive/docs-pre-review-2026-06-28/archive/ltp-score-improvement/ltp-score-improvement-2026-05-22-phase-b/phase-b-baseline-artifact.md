- `clock_nanosleep02`, `nanosleep01`: demoted after RV stable103 targeted `TFAIL` in Phase A.
- `sched_getscheduler02`: RV clean historically, but LA musl `TFAIL` blocks promotion.
- `clock_gettime01`, `clock_nanosleep01`, `gettimeofday02`: timeout risk blocks promotion unless fresh evidence is clean and bounded.
